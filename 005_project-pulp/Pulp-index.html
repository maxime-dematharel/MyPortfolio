PULP (ex blacksheep)

Product Owner / UX Designer

october-december 2019

Blacksheep was founded in 2018 by 3 HEC students, with the aim to simplify lunch break by creating a digital menu ordering system. Arnaud, Olivier, and Antoine, the 3 founders, wanted to help restaurant stop losing customers due to the waiting time.

My focus at blacksheep was to understand the value proposition that the customers prefere in our product. This will allow us to develop the best product needed and find product/market fit.

BRIEF OVERVIEW

I've worked as a full time Product Owner for blacksheep during 6 months. I was hire in order to identify the High Expectation Customers and adapt blacksheep offer to the user's need. I helped blacksheep in their transition from B2B to B2C. I worked on their brand identity, organise a product backlog, sprint and design a brand new product call Pulp. I organised the launch of Pulp and find beta testeurs in order to test the MVP and continue iterate.
