BOUILLANT!

December 2019

Bouillant! is a young startup created around the love of home cooking. Bouillant! proposes sharing cooking lesson experiences from particular passionate peoples to curious customers.
Founded by Julie and Thomas, Bouillant! aim to share passion about homemade food and grand-mother recipe. They are highlighting a homemade cooking, accessible to all and easily reproducible at home in terms of ingredients and materials used.

BRIEF OVERVIEW

I've worked as freelancer for a 2 weeks sprint. I was asked to create a plateform for Bouillant! customer so that they could create and manage their own experiences easily. In two weeks, I've ran interviews and usability tests with my prototype in order to deliver a high-fidelity MVP ready to develop. I've followed Design Thinking approach in order to empathize and proposed a solution wanted by the user. I've proposed a new user flow and built a MVP on Figma.
